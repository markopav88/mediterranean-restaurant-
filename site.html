Welcome to Our Mediterranean Restaurant

Indulge in the rich flavors of the Mediterranean with our delicious dishes. Our restaurant is run by a group of friends out of Marist College that aim to reach the local population with our authentic Mediterranean food.

We are a group of college students attempting to create a website for our Mediterranean Restaurant! Founded in 2023, we are a very new business and we look forward to meeting our customers!

A random idea turned into a reality when we decided to band together and completely go forward with the plan of opening a restaurant

We believe that food brings people together and makes them happy. We also think trying new foods is something everybody should get into.

Our mission is to deliver the best Mediterranean food experience to our customers and support our community
